Before You Write

Common questions

Submissions

Can I submit a recipe or article?

Lean Theory is currently written by one person, but I'd genuinely welcome input from qualified people — especially registered dietitians, doctors, and certified trainers who spot something I've gotten wrong or could cover better. Send a short note and I'll follow up.

Corrections

I think an article has an error

Please include the article title, the specific claim, and a source if you have one. We review and update Facts Panels and articles regularly.

Programs

Which program should I start with?

Tell us your current routine, available time, and equipment, and we'll point you to the program whose Facts Panel matches your situation.
